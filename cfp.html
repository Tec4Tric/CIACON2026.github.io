Call for papers (Flyer)

The CIACON 2026 program spans two days and features a diverse activities including paper presentations, expert talks, and panel discussions. Professionals, researchers, faculty members, and students from various backgrounds are warmly welcomed to engage in this enriching conference experience.

 

All accepted and presented papers will be included in conference proceedings and published in IEEE Xplore.

 

Authors interested in contributing to the conference are kindly asked to submit their manuscripts in PDF format, limited to 6 pages on A4 size paper, via the Microsoft CMT submission portal

Please note that CIACON 2026 will exclusively review full papers that fall within the conference's scope.

Benefits

  • All Accepted and Presented Papers will be submitted to IEEE Xplore for consideration to be published. (Scopus Indexed).
  • Awards will be given for the best research papers.

CIACON 2026: A Hybrid Conference

CIACON 2026 will feature a mix of hybrid experiences. Full paper presentations will be divided into in-person presenter-only sessions and virtual presenter-only sessions, as determined by the organizers' discretion based on valid reasons. Both in-person and virtual sessions will be streamed and recorded on an appropriate platform. Authors were required to make their selection between these options during their camera-ready submission.
