The 2026 International Conference on Computing, Intelligence, and Applications (CIACON 2026) is to create a dynamic platform for cross-sector dialogues and the exchange of breakthrough ideas across a variety of challenging interdisciplinary areas. This premier international event aims to advance existing knowledge, address unsolved problems relevant to the common mass, and explore new applications and opportunities in the interconnected fields of computing and artificial intelligence. By bringing together researchers, practitioners, and industry leaders from academia, research institutions, and industries, the conference will foster collaboration, promote top-level research, and focus on recent outstanding achievements, future trends, emerging requirements, and real-world applications in these domains.

About the Institution

Dr. B. C. Roy Engineering College, Durgapur (An Autonomous Institute), popularly known as BCREC, was set up on 21st August, 2000, under the management of Dr. B. C. Roy Engineering College Society. The institute is dedicated to the memory of Dr. Bidhan Chandra Roy, the architect of modern West Bengal. The institute offers a variety of B. Tech programmes in engineering like CSE, IT, ECE, Computer Science and Design (CSD), CSE in AIML, EE, CSE in Data Science, CSE in Cyber Security, ME and CE, and also offers programmes like M.Tech, MBA and MCA. The institute has earned NBA accreditation for B.Tech CSE, IT, EE, ECE and ME disciplines and is also accredited by NAAC.

About the Department

The Department of Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) at Dr. B C Roy Engineering College has been a cornerstone of the institute since its inception in 2000. Accredited by the NBA four times, most recently for the period 2023–2026, the department delivers outcome-based education through its B.Tech and M.Tech programmes, with annual intakes of 180 and 18 students, respectively.

The department prides itself on its state-of-the-art infrastructure, including well-equipped laboratories, a dedicated library, seminar rooms, and digital classrooms that support a rich learning environment. The faculty, comprising experts from prestigious institutions, combines academic rigor with industry experience to provide a holistic education. They are actively involved in research and frequently publish in leading national and international journals and conferences, contributing to the academic and professional growth of the department.

The Department of CSE has an excellent placement record, with graduates securing positions in top-tier companies such as Capgemini, TCS, Infosys, IBM, Wipro, and many others. Additionally, a significant number of students pursue higher studies in reputed institutions in India and abroad, facilitated by their achievements in competitive exams like GATE and GRE.

To foster a culture of innovation and collaboration, the department regularly organises symposia, conferences, workshops, and seminars. These events offer students and faculty opportunities to network with industry leaders, researchers, and academicians, broadening their perspectives and enhancing their skills.

Committed to academic excellence and the all-round development of its students, the Department of Computer Science & Engineering prepares graduates to excel as professionals, researchers, and leaders on both national and global platforms.
